✅ Commands:

    !boss died <boss> → Register the death of an Epic boss.

    !bossstatus → Shows the current status of all bosses.

    !cancelboss <boss (GMT-3)> → Cancels the registration of a boss.

    !setboss <boss> <time> → Manually sets the time of the boss's death. (Queen Ant has to be written together for it to work. e.g: queenant)

    !nextrespawn → Shows the next respawn among all bosses.

    !listbosses → Lists the available bosses.
